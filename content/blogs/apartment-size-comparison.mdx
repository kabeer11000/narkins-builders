# 2 Bedroom vs 3 Bedroom: Which Apartment Size is Right for You?

Choosing the right apartment size is crucial for both comfort and investment returns. Here's everything you need to know about 2BR vs 3BR apartments in Bahria Town.

## Size and Layout Comparison

### 2 Bedroom Apartments
- **Size**: 1,200-1,400 sq ft
- **Ideal for**: Young couples, small families, investors
- **Price range**: PKR 2.5-3.2 Crore

### 3 Bedroom Apartments  
- **Size**: 1,600-1,800 sq ft
- **Ideal for**: Growing families, those needing home office
- **Price range**: PKR 3.5-4.5 Crore

## Our Recommendations

### Choose 2 Bedroom If:
- First-time property buyer
- Looking for higher rental yields
- Want quicker resale potential
- Budget under PKR 3.5 Crore

### Choose 3 Bedroom If:
- Family with children
- Need home office space
- Looking for maximum appreciation
- Budget allows for premium investment
